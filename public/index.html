Your source for information about the crime in Philadelphia
PhillyNow is a catalog of heat maps of different types of crime in the Philadelphia region. We are able to display informational heat maps that can allow Philadelphia residents to visualize the criminal activity in their area. Utilizing this website, a user can become safer with more knowledge of high crime areas.

Stage 2

During the next stage of the hackathon, we dedicated all of our time to developing the project. We started with barebone front-end and back-end and developed it further with each addition. We were able to overcome every error we encountered and by 5 am Eastern Pacific Time, we were able to complete the backend of the website.

Stage 1

In the first Stage of the Hackathon, we began brainstorming and planning out ways to tackle our idea. We first began with dividing up our roles and creating a roadmap that we would have to follow until the end of the event.

Stage 3

During the final stretch, we began the testing and submitting phase. This stage felt relieving and satisfying for all of us as it was the culmination of our hard work. We chose our initial idea because we believe that the community in Philadelphia needs to be aware of the type criminal activity in the neighbourhoods around them. With this information, a community member can be smarter about their location of travel and be more aware of what's going on around them. Creating a solution is first recognizin the problem, and with the data we gathered, we can allow for Philadelphia residents to be aware of the problem in their community.
